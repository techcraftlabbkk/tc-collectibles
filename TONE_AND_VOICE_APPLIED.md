## **CHANGES APPLIED BY PAGE**

### **1️⃣ HOMEPAGE**
```
Before: "Authentic PSA Pokémon Cards
        Verified. Graded. Shipped from Bangkok. 
        The only marketplace you trust for premium collectibles."

After: "Authentic PSA Pokémon Cards
       Verified. Graded. Shipped from Bangkok."
```
**Reason:** Removed wordy trust claim ("the only marketplace you trust..."). Shorter = more confident.

**Featured Products Section:**
```
Before: "Featured Cards
        Hand-picked gems. Verified authentic. Ready to own."

After: "Premium Cards
       Verified authentic. Graded. Ready to collect."
```
**Reason:** "Premium" is cleaner than "Featured". "Collect" is friendlier than "own".

## **TONE CHECKLIST FOR FUTURE COPY**

When adding new text to the site, follow this checklist:

- [ ] Is it under 10 words where possible?
- [ ] Does it sound like a friend, not a salesman?
- [ ] Is there any word I can remove and still be clear?
- [ ] Am I making a claim I don't need to make?
- [ ] Would an emoji help? (use sparingly)
- [ ] Would a different verb be friendlier?
- [ ] Does it match the TC Collectibles personality?
