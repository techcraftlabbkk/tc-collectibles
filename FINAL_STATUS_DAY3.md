## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM IMPLEMENTED

**Color Scheme:**
- Primary Gradient: Purple (#667eea) → Indigo → Blue
- Accent Buttons: Amber (#f59e0b) with gradient effects
- Text: Gray-900 (bold headings), Gray-600 (supporting text)
- Borders: Purple-200, Purple-300 (2px)
- Shadows: Enhanced on hover (shadow-lg, shadow-2xl)
- Status: Green (available), Red (sold out)

**Typography:**
- Headings: font-black, text-4xl-7xl
- Bold text: font-bold, font-semibold
- Labels: text-xs-sm, uppercase, tracking-widest
- Consistent line-height and letter-spacing

**Components:**
- Buttons: Gradient backgrounds, hover states, active scale
- Forms: Purple borders, focus rings, larger inputs
- Cards: White background, 2px purple borders, rounded-2xl
- Gradients: to-br (to bottom-right), multiple color stops
- Spacing: Consistent gaps (6px-12px), padding (4px-8px)
